Digital instructions for optimizing the cleaning process

Instructions created for the cleaning company to optimize the workflow for cleaners and managers.

I created instructions in Polish and Russian and implemented them on Webflow.

Problem. New cleaners struggle to remember what to clean next and how to clean it, requiring managers to spend extra time explaining and checking the cleaning process.

Solution. Step-by-step digital instructions to set cleaning standards and answer: “What to clean?” and “How to clean?”
