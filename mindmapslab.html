Website and branding for MindMapsLab

MindMapsLab is a growing organization that currently offers educational products on personal efficiency techniques. The company's first website was created by the founder on WordPress, but it became outdated.

My task was to present the products more informatively to the target audience, improve navigation, and create a new visual design.

mindmapslab.pl ↗
Implemented on Webflow by me

Additionally, the client planned to promote the products through social media and free educational materials on the website.

Website structure

I created a structure where each page has its own use case. It helps promote products separately on social media or blogs, and makes navigation more intuitive.

Academy Website

Academy is a course designed to improve learning, analysis, planning, and creativity skills through the mind mapping technique. The course will be beneficial for people who want to improve these skills for career growth or personal development.

I developed a deep understanding of the topic and wrote all the content on the website in Polish, designed a responsive visual layout, and implemented it on Webflow. I also met with the client weekly to present solutions and align expectations, ensuring everyone was satisfied with the outcome.
