Website and branding for MindMapsLab

MindMapsLab is a growing organization that currently offers educational products on personal efficiency techniques. The company's first website was created by the founder on WordPress, but it became outdated.

My task was to present the products more informatively to the target audience, improve navigation, and create a new visual design.

Website structure

The client planned to promote products through social media and blog. I created a structure where each page has its own use case, which helps to promote the products separately and makes navigation intuitive.

Academy Website

MindMapsLab Academy is a course designed to improve learning, analysis, planning, and creativity skills through the mind mapping technique. The course will be beneficial for people who want to improve these skills for career growth or personal development.

I developed an understanding of the topic and the target audience's problems, wrote all the website content in Polish, designed a responsive visual layout, and implemented it on Webflow. Additionally, I met with the client weekly to present solutions and align expectations, ensuring everyone was satisfied with the outcome.
