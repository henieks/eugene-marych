Research & Findings

As part of the research, I conducted an interview with the CEO to learn more about the company, its goals, target audience, frequently asked questions, and how the service itself works.

To extend the gathered information with new insights, I also conducted an analysis of the company's competitors and reviews from their customers on forums. Based on this, I identified the most important customer questions:

Question 1
What will be cleaned?
Question 2
How much will it cost?
Question 3
How to order the cleaning?
Question 4
What if I am not satisfied with the cleaning?
Question 5
What if the cleaner damages something in the apartment?
Question 6
Are the cleaning products safe for health?

Website concept

Based on the information I obtained during the research, I developed a website content concept which helped me latter to design better visual and functional solutions.

Solutions

Cleaning options

There are 2 primary cleaning options: regular and deep. For clarity, I've listed what each option includes based on different apartment areas.

Cleaning price calculation

Because there are many factors that affect pricing, it's tough for clients to figure out the cost. So, I made a calculator that makes it simple to find out how much cleaning a specific apartment would cost.

To create the calculator, I looked at competitor's solutions and with the client's assistance, determined what factors would affect pricing and how. I made a trial version in Excel, and then used JavaScript to put it on the website.

Additional services

Some cleaning services do not have a fixed price and cannot be included in the main cleaning plans. Therefore, I have provided the option to order them additionally or separately.

Ecological cleaning

Based on customer support ticket data, some people preferred chemical-free cleaning. That's why I introduced the option for steam-only cleaning.

User-generated content

I added customer reviews to confirm the quality of the cleaning service, and the link to Google reviews for further verification.

Ordering the service

I accomplished this using the sales lead form, because it's easy to implement, and it eliminates the need to provide an option for logging into accounts or dealing with a more complicated backend.

Ordering a cleaning service via phone or SMS enables more accurate pricing. Based on the desk research, SMS or messenger options make ordering process more comfortable for specific clients.

Stepper above the form helps clients to be aware of the ordering process.

Style guide & Components

Logo concept

TiBiSi Group offers a variety of services, including cleaning, laundry and apartment rental. Therefore, the goal was to incorporate other services into the logo design for the future needs.

The color and bottom caption are customized for each service, while the rest remains unchanged to maintain consistency across the brand.
