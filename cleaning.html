Identification & landing page for apartment cleaning service
The Challenge: The cleaning company needed to promote their services for private clients â€” people who need cleaning in their apartment or house.
The Outcome: Landing page with ordering form and Google Ads campaign.

Desktop. 1280px

Assumptions

1
Based on the desk research and interview with the client, the most important customers questions are:
What will be cleaned?
How much will it cost?
How to order the cleaning?
What if I am not satisfied with the cleaning?
What if the cleaner damages something in the apartment?
Are the cleaning products safe for health?
2
For some customers it is not convenient to order cleaning services over the phone.

Limitation

The user interaction should be easy to implement. Without the use of databases.

Problems & Solutions

1
The price for cleaning depends on a variety of factors. That's why I have solved this by using a simple calculator that allows for an accurate calculation of the cleaning price.
2
I processed the service orders through a registration form. This method is convenient as it is not difficult to implement and allows for a more precise customization of the service according to the customer's needs, as well as a more accurate pricing.
3
To make the ordering process more comfortable for the majority of customers, I also added the option to place orders via SMS.

Mobile version. 375px

Logo concept

TiBiSi Group offers a variety of services, including cleaning, laundry and apartment rental. Therefore, the goal was to incorporate other services into the logo design for the future needs.
The color and bottom caption are customized for each service, while the rest remains unchanged to maintain consistency across the brand.
