Problems & Solutions

Cleaning options
There are 2 primary cleaning options: regular and deep. For clarity, I've listed what each option includes based on different apartment areas.
Cleaning price calculation
The price for cleaning depends on a variety of factors. To address this, I have developed a simple calculator that accurately calculates the cleaning price.
Additional services
Some cleaning services do not have a fixed price and cannot be included in the main cleaning plans. Therefore, I have provided the option to order them additionally or separately.
Ecological cleaning
Based on customer support ticket data, some people preferred chemical-free cleaning. That's why I introduced the option for steam-only cleaning.
User-generated content
I added customer reviews to confirm the quality of the cleaning service, and the link to Google reviews for further verification.
Ordering the service
I accomplished this using the sales lead form, because it's easy to implement, and it eliminates the need to provide an option for logging into accounts or dealing with a more complicated backend.
Ordering a cleaning service via phone or SMS enables more accurate pricing. SMS or messenger options make ordering process more comfortable for specific clients.
Stepper above the form helps clients to be aware of the ordering process.

Logo concept

TiBiSi Group offers a variety of services, including cleaning, laundry and apartment rental. Therefore, the goal was to incorporate other services into the logo design for the future needs.
The color and bottom caption are customized for each service, while the rest remains unchanged to maintain consistency across the brand.
