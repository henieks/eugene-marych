Landing page & identification for apartment cleaning service

Context
Project for cleaning company. Focused on people who needs cleaning in there apartment or house.
Goal
Sell apartment cleaning services.
Outcome
Landing page with the cleaning service ordering form.

Desktop. 1280px

Assumptions

1
Based on the desk research and interview with the client, the most important customer's questions are:
What will be cleaned?
How much will it cost?
How to order the cleaning?
What if I am not satisfied with the cleaning?
What if the cleaner damages something in the apartment?
Are the cleaning products safe for health?
